_hyperscript Multilingual Adapter

Writing hyperscript in Spanish, Japanese, Korean, Chinese, and French — powered by the original _hyperscript runtime

Toggle ES - Spanish

Click the button to toggle the .active class:

_="alternar .active"

Toggle JA - Japanese

Click to toggle .active class:

_=".active を 切り替え"

Toggle KO - Korean

_=".active 을 토글"

Add Class FR - French

Click to add .highlight to the box:

_="ajouter .highlight sur #frenchBox"
Box

Toggle ZH - Chinese

_="切换 .active"

Put ES - Spanish

Click to put text into the message area:

_='poner "Hola desde _hyperscript!" en #msg'
(message will appear here)

Remove Class ES - Spanish

Click "Agregar" to add, then "Quitar" to remove .highlight:

_="quitar .highlight de #removeBox"
Box

Compound Command ES - Spanish

Two commands chained with then — add highlight, then put text:

_='agregar .highlight en #compoundBox then poner "Done!" en #compoundMsg'
Box
(message appears here)

English Control EN - English

Standard English hyperscript (no translation needed):

_="on click toggle .active on me"

Inherited Language ES - Inherited

This section has data-hyperscript-lang="es" on the parent. All children inherit Spanish:

Parent: data-hyperscript-lang="es"
